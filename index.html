Chat History Analyzer

Welcome to the Chat History Analyzer! Follow the steps below to participate:

Instructions:

  1. Download your WhatsApp chat history with media. A guide on how to do this is available here. This will be a zip file; please extract the text file from here.
  2. Open an incognito browser and create another tab with this website. This ensures complete transparency as no personal data is stored.
  3. Turn off the Wifi/Internet on your device. This is an additional precaution to ensure your privacy.
  4. Once your internet is off, press the button below to proceed to the analyzer page and upload your extracted text file. Allow the file to be read by our parser.
  5. Still offline, download the resulting file or copy and paste the results.
  6. Once you have your results saved, close the incognito tab and reconnect to the internet.
  7. Email the resulting file (or copied text) to [[email here]] with the subject line “WhatsApp Chat Data”.

And you are all done! Thank you once more for your help.
